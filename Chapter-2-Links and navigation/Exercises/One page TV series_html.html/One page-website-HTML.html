A riveting tale of the iconic villain's origin,
showcasing her transformation from an ambitious young

Storyline

Cruella" follows Estella's evolution from a young fashion designer to the iconic villain, Cruella de Vil, in 1970s London. As she navigates the world of high fashion,
embracing her darker side, she plots to dominate the industry. Along the way, she faces challenges and the consequences of her actions, revealing the power of self-discovery.
