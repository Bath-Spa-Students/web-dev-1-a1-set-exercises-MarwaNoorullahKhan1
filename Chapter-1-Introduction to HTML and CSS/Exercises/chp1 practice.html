SHRIMP NOODLE SOUP

LETS GET COOKING

  1. 1 teaspoons vegetable oil
  2. 1 onion, chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
  5. 1 pinch crushed red pepper
  6. 2 quarts chicken broth
  7. 1 cup peeled, diagonally sliced carrots
  8. 1 cup diagonally sliced celery
  9. 2 cups snow peas
  10. 12 ounces fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  11. 4 ounces rice vermicelli
  12. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  13. ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  1. Season shrimp with salt then heat up a pan or wok over high heat
  2. Cook your shrimp for 1-2 minutes and remove to add back later.
  3. Prepare your noodles according to the packaging.
  4. Usually they are pre-cooked so I just needed to soak my noodles in hot water for 1-2 minutes then drain.
  5. Cut all your vegetables,
  6. I thinly slice onions, rough chop garlic, broccoli and cabbage.
  7. Heat your wok over high heat with 1 tbsp of oil then add all your vegetables and cook until softened about 5 minutes.
  8. Add your drained noodles along with the pre-mixed sauce and continue to mix
  9. Mix until the sauce has been fully absorbed by your noodles.
  10. Add the shrimp and cooked edamame at the very end, give it a quick mix and enjoy!
  11. I top with sesame seeds and green onions.
